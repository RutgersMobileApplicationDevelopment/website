Who we are

RUMAD is the mobile app development club at Rutgers. We run a 2 semester program to teach mobile app development to beginers called the Accelerator.
We also have a bi-weekly speaker series where engineers and product managers give tech talks called How it's Built.
Our first general interest meeting will be on September 10th in the Busch Student Center room 122 ABC from 7pm-8pm, we really hope to see you there!
