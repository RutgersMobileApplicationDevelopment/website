Who we are

RUMAD is the mobile app development club at Rutgers. We run a 2 semester program to teach mobile app development to beginers called the Accelerator.
We have bi-weekly speaker series where engineers and product managers explain things called How it's Built.
Our first general interest meeting will be on September 10th in the BSC from 7pm-8pm, we really hope to see you there!
